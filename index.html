Rob Arcand is a writer, editor, research assistant, and occasional freelance web developer living between Durham, NC and Brooklyn.

For the last few years, he's worked as a teaching assistant at Duke University and a news editor at SPIN Magazine, publishing work on the political economy of digital media and the hidden labor behind many of today's largest tech platforms.

More recently, he's been brushing up on his dev skills at Thinkful, where he's studying React, Node, and other front-end frameworks in the interest of pursuing future roles as a full-stack developer.
